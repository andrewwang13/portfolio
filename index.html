About

Andrew L. Wang is a journalist and photographer based in Los Angeles.

Andrew explores his love of communities and nature through photography. He started making photos with an old Pentax 35mm when he was a kid, switched to digital sometime in the early 2000s and got his first iPhone in 2010. These days he primarily does street photography with travel and outdoors work mixed in.

He shoots small events on a freelance basis. For more information, contact him at:

instagram | twitter | email

A note about Ren Ai Photography's name: Andrew's Chinese name is 王立仁 (Wang Liren). Chinese speakers often ask which ren, and the best explanation is 仁爱的仁, the ren in ren ai. Ren ai is a Confucian virtue that roughly translates to benevolence or kindness.
