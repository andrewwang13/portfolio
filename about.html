About Ren Ai Photography

Andrew L. Wang is a journalist and photographer based in Los Angeles.

Andrew explores his love of communities and nature through photography. He started making photos as a kid with a Pentax 35mm and switched to digital in the early 2000s. These days he primarily shoots with a Nikon D7200 with 35mm/1.8 and 11-16mm/2.8 lenses and an iPhone 6s Plus.

His work consists predominantly of street photography with travel and outdoors work mixed in.

Andrew also shoots small events on a freelance basis. For more information, contact him at:

instagram | twitter | email

A note about Ren Ai Photography: Andrew's Chinese name is 王立仁 (Wang Liren). Chinese speakers often ask which ren, and the best explanation is 仁爱的仁, the ren in ren ai. Ren ai is a Confucian virtue that roughly translates to benevolence or kindness.
